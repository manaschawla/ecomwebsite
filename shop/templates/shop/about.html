About My Awesome Cart

My Awesome Cart is a growing Indian e-commerce brand offering curated products across diverse categories like electronics, fashion, and essentials. We are committed to making online shopping simpler, faster, and more affordable for everyone.

Since our inception in 2025, we have focused on quality, affordability, and great customer service. What began as a simple idea has evolved into a full-fledged online platform trusted by thousands. Our goal is to deliver happiness to your doorstep—one product at a time.

Meet Our Founder

Manas Chawla, the founder of My Awesome Cart, is a passionate entrepreneur with a clear mission—to revolutionize online shopping in India. With a background in technology and business, Manas brings both innovation and empathy to the table.

His vision has shaped the company's strong values: customer satisfaction, honesty, and ease of access. Under his leadership, the company continues to grow with a focus on trust and service excellence.
